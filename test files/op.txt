<h2>Multiples of 3 and 5</h2><div id="problem_info" class="info"><h3>Problem 1</h3><span>Published on Friday, 5th October 2001, 06:00 pm; Solved by 507142; Difficulty rating: 5%</span></div>&#13;
<div class="problem_content" role="problem">&#13;
<p>If we list all the natural numbers below 10 that are multiples of 3 or 5, we get 3, 5, 6 and 9. The sum of these multiples is 23.</p>
<p>Find the sum of all the multiples of 3 or 5 below 1000.</p>
